Kuching
Kuching, the capital city of Sarawak in Malaysia, is a vibrant blend of history, culture, and nature. Nestled along the banks of the Sarawak River, this charming city offers a mix of colonial architecture, modern urban spaces, and traditional markets. It's renowned for its cat-themed landmarks, paying homage to its name, which means "cat" in Malay. Visitors are drawn to Kuching for its rich cultural heritage, reflected in the diverse communities of Malay, Chinese, and indigenous peoples.
