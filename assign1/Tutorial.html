Tutorial

Step by step tutorial on how to transfer a fresh leaf into herbarium specimens

Picking Location and Collecting Leaves

First step to start your herbarium journey would be by collecting leaves, and picking suitable location will ease your work. The location examples are

  1. Park
  2. Garden
  3. Meadow
  4. Forest

Then, when it come to leaves collecting, make sure you collect fresh leaves instead of withered one. Withered leaves are easy to chip and fragile to handle hence not suitable for herbarium.

Preparing the leaves

After reaching home, wash the leaves with water to remove dust and dirt. Then, remove excess moisture by wiping the leaves with a paper towel

Pressing the leaves

Press the leaves hard
